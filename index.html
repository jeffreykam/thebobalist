Top 10 Boba Shops in San Francisco

Many boba shops have opened in San Francisco over the latter half of the last decade. Compared to it's neighboring cities in the south bay, San Francisco only had a few lackluster options for boba (Ahem.. purple kow). Today, there are plenty of great options for boba. Here are the top 10:

  1. Black Sugar
  2. Asha Tea House
  3. Plentea
  4. Little Sweet
  5. Boba Guys
  6. Teaspoon
  7. CoCo Fresh
  8. Urban Ritual
  9. Steap Tea Bar
  10. Yi Fang
